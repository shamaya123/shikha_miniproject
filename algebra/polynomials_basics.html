Example 1: Simplify (3u + 5w)(3u – 5w)

Using the algebraic identities (a + b)(a - b) = a2-b2, we substitute a for 3u and b for 5w.

(3u + 5w)(3u – 5w)

= (3u)2 – (5w)2

= 9u2 – 25w2

Thus (3u + 5w)(3u – 5w) = 9u2 – 25w2
